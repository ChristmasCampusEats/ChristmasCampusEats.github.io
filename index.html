Christmas Campus Eats

About Events

It's this time of year again folks, and campus is getting ready for Christmas. Among other things there's the Christmas market both on Campus and in Leamington, and a number of Christmas balls and events on campus. We're here to make your life easier by helping you choose where you can find the best festive food in between all the festivities! Below are a number of links to the best finds on campus.

The Bread Oven

The week 10 Christmas dinner sandwich is back: Goat’s cheese, sweet potato and cranberry sauce for all veggies out there, and turkey, gravy, stuffing and cranberry for anyone feeling extra hungry. If you’re trying to detox before Christmas hits, you can always opt for it in salad form or a half sandwich! Ho ho hold my debit card so I don't spend all my money!

Menu

Canopy

Canopy, our new and favourite green restaurant on campus, is also providing its own list of Christmas specials. A set 3 course menu is £16.95 and looks incredible. There are two Christmas special pizzas, one with sweet potato, stuffing and caramelised onions, and the other wish chipolatas and crispy bacon. The bowls on offer involve vegan terrine and a beetroot goats cheese salad.

Menu

Curiositea

Come to curiositea for some seasonal treats including tasty praline brownie tea, Rudolph the Red Nose Mocha, Mr Frosty’s Freakshake and others! (ps. don’t miss out on the Baileys hot chocolate and mulled wine for a daytime drink). If drinks aren’t your thing then they have a selection of other foods for you to try including yule log, mince pies and Christmas bakewells

Menu

Dirty Duck

Not only does the Dirty duck have a Christmas quiz this Thursday to get you in the Christmas mood. It is also providing some seasonal specials to compliment that beer you know you’re going to need at the end of week 10. You can help yourself to a Roast Turkey Dinner, a Turkey Burger or a Butternut Squash Wellington all for the affordable student prices the duck is known for.

Menu

Pret A Manager

Pret with its bright lights and glass windows makes you feel like you’re outside enjoying the crisp air whilst still being cosy and warm. As always, they have their Classic and Veggie Christmas sandwich filled with everything you could want, including crispy onions and port and orange cranberry sauce. They cater to every dietary requirement so you’re guaranteed to find something you enjoy.

Menu

Varsity

For Christmas, Varsity is providing a buffet menu and a festive fayre menu. The former provides pigs in blankets, breaded brie wedges, sage and onion stuffing balls among other things. All for £12.49 a person. With the option for cookie crumb profiteroles at the end. The festive fayre menu provides three courses for £17.49, and there is lots to choose from! Plus you can get a traditional Christmas pudding.
